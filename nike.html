Nike Run Music

Enhancing running performance through personalized music

nike run music is a feature concept designed to increase runner motivation by aligning music rhythm with running cadence. what began as a personal curiosity evolved into a full ux/ui proposal for the nike run club app.

Context

Why this matter to me

running has always been part of my routine, and nike run is the app i commonly use to track my runs. this project began from my own experience noticing how music influences my motivation and cadence — and wondering why the app didn't already do something about it.

Research

Listening before designing

i interviewed four runners with different habits and goals. across every conversation, one theme repeated: music matters, but no app was making it work with the run. the gap between music apps and running apps was the brief.

The Feature

Music that moves at your rhythm

the feature works in three ways: it suggests playlists matched to your workout type, visualizes how each song synced with your pace after the run, and explains the science behind bpm ranges and running zones in context.
